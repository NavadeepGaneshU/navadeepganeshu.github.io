Hi there, I'm Ayoub!

I'm a young gradute at Polytech Sorbonne in Paris, specialized in the field of Embedded Systems. I'm italian with moroccan roots and I have been living in France for five years now. I see myself as the result of mixing several cultures.

I'm very passionated about technology and innovation, especially in the domains of electronics and computer science. This is one of the reasons that lead me to choose embedded systems as a major for my studies, since it combines these two fields blending hardware and software. During my studies and school projects I have discovered that I really enjoy low-level programming: it's funny to go deeper into processors architectures and build designs from scratch.

During my internships I've had the opportunity to work in the field of IoT, developing custom IoT solutions for agriculture. During my last experience at STMicroelectronics, I've even joined a team which develop and maintain a C-written middleware that permits STM32 microcontrollers to send and receive data through cellular networks (such as NB-IoT, LTE Cat. M, GPRS, ...).

These experiences gave me a deeper understanding of this technology, the benefits brought by it and taught me the methodology for the design process. IoT systems may appear simpler than others; this is partly true because often we don't have real-time costraints, but the architecture still remains complex needing skills on both low level (bare-metal development) and high level layers (OSI model). You can find more details about my work experience here.
