Hi there, I'm Navadeep!

I'm currently an Electrical Engineering undergraduate student at Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences, Bengaluru.

I'm passionate about technology and research with major interests in Embedded Systems, Circuit Design, Analog and Power Electronics. Applying these, I like designing, prototyping devices and realize technology and its applications through this. Occasionally, RF and Quantum stuffs fascinate me (yes, the Starlink, 6G and CERN).

After taking an undergraduate course in Electrical Engineering, with the curiosity to explore areas and work hands-on, during my first year I worked as a Hardware IoT intern with an AgriTech startup for devising agricultural field sensor nodes. Then, as a research intern at the Centre for Energy, IIT Guwahati where I worked on electrical load data modelling and prediction for exploring ways to optimize energy generation and utilization. I am interested to work in an environment having a mix of research and implementation factor around ideas. Currently, I am working as an Embedded Hardware intern with a CleanTech startup for developing technological solutions to help restore the air around us.

I strongly believe in open-source culture and have the motive of making technology more easily and affordably expand. I enjoy being a part of technical communities and societies and an active member at Element14 and Hackster where I involve in building theme projects, design challenges, discussions and RoadTest program through which I get to work hands-on with electronic evaluation kits and experiment around discrete components.

I write blogs related to technology and application projects at CL3VERTRONICS-spark something!
