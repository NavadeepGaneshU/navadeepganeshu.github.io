Hi there, I'm Navadeep!

I am a hobbyist electronics + software developer and a tinkerer.

Graduating from Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences, Bengaluru in Electrical Engineering, I currently work at Astrome in the electronic design domain. My interests lie around Embedded Systems and Power Electronics. Occasionally, RF and Quantum stuffs fascinate me (yes, the Starlink, 6G and CERN).

Taking up an undergraduate course in Electrical Engineering, with the curiosity to explore engineering domains and go hands-on, during my first year I worked as a Hardware IoT intern with an AgriTech startup for devising agricultural sensors. Then, as a research intern at the Centre for Energy, IIT Guwahati and went on working with a bunch of startups in climate tech and battery systems domain, all-hands electronics! I am interested working in an environment having a mix of research and implementation factor around ideas. Thoroughly enjoying this for 5+ year now, I am also an active member at a bunch of technical communities including Element14 Community and DevHeads where I involve in building theme projects, discussions and RoadTest programs.

The amusement to all stuff dates back to my very childhood days. This brings back intense nostalgia - fiddling with what's inside the toys, breaking apart portable lamps at home and relentlessly experimenting by trial and error. I write blogs related to technology and my random musings here at Blogs Page!.
